Your Study Abroad Roadmap

Follow our step-by-step guide to navigate the journey from application to arrival at your dream university.

Research & Planning

Explore universities, programs, and funding options. Connect with alumni to understand their experiences.

1
12-18 months before enrollment
  • Research universities and programs
  • Connect with alumni mentors
  • Prepare for standardized tests (GRE, GMAT, etc.)
10-12 months before enrollment
  • Prepare application materials
  • Write Statement of Purpose and essays
  • Request recommendation letters
2

Application Preparation

Create compelling application materials, including personal statements, essays, and recommendation letters.

Application Submission

Submit applications to your chosen universities and track their status.

3
8-10 months before enrollment
  • Submit university applications
  • Pay application fees
  • Track application status
4-6 months before enrollment
  • Accept admission offer
  • Apply for scholarships and financial aid
  • Prepare for visa application
4

Admission & Funding

Evaluate admission offers, secure funding, and prepare for the next steps.

Visa & Pre-Departure

Apply for your student visa, arrange accommodation, and prepare for your journey.

5
2-3 months before enrollment
  • Attend visa interview
  • Arrange accommodation
  • Book flights and prepare for travel
